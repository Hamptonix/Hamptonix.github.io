About the Server

OdyCraft is a School Minecraft Server hosted by Hamptonix. OdyCraft has a huge community of 50+ Students!

📜 Server Rules

Read and follow all rules. Joining the OdyCraft Server means you automatically agree to abide by these rules.

Rule 01 — Core
This server is an SMP, meaning that you are allowed to build, destroy, and interact with the world as you please.
Rule 02 — No Cheats
No freecam, wall viewing, or xraying.
Rule 03 — Destruction
Griefing, stealing, rioting, is allowed but not encouraged.
Rule 04 — Server Health
No lag machines or attacks that hurt the server.
Rule 05 — Family Members
Family members are allowed, but if the school attendee gets banned, they also get banned.
Rule 06 — Registration
Family members not attending Odyssey must state their real name, relationship, and age in the Discord Server or will face a permanent ban.
Rule 07 — No Alts
No alternate accounts allowed.
Rule 08 — NSFW
Any NSFW content in any channel results in a lifetime ban from all OdyCraft related services.
⚠ Punishment Guidelines
  • Punishment lengths are determined by staff at their discretion.
  • Second offences will always result in a permanent ban, no matter the circumstance.
  • All players who are pardoned will be cleared and reset to spawn.
